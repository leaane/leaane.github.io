My name is Leane, and I'm a second-year computer science student at Pasadena City College. I'm currently in the process of transferring to a 4-year university (pray for me).

My tech journey started in high school. I was never really tech-savvy (still not, I'm practically illiterate in Windows) and was reluctant to use technology as a child. However, I decided it was finally time to learn because technology is meant to make your life easier. I joined my school's newspaper and learned not only journalism but also design with Adobe products. Then, I took AP Computer Science Principles and realized this subject was something I wanted to pursue. I reveled in understanding how things work and being able to build programs. From learning simple keyboard shortcuts to creating my own website, I was able to discover my passion for computer science, work jobs related to that field, and learn not out of obligation but genuine enthusiasm.

Since 2021, I've been part of an initiative committed to fostering digital equity in underserved communities called Delete the Divide. I come from a low-income, immigrant family, so it's been amazing to have the opportunity to develop myself while simultaneously helping others like me. I've worked as a full-stack web developer, IT support, and digital assistant. I love being able to undertake projects that give me purpose. For example, I did data entry and made a program to locate books based on Accelerated Reader levels and points for the library.

But, I'm not all just about work. You only live once, so I strive to live life to the fullest. As a result, a work-life balance and a company's work culture are extremely important to me. I love the outdoors and spending quality time with loved ones. I enjoy hiking, escape rooms, rafting, and stuff around that sort. I also enjoy more tame things such as trying new restaurants, doing cute arts and crafts, and having a board game night. If I just want to relax, I'm obsessed with bothering my wittle guinea pigs, binging c-dramas/k-dramas, and listening to alternative/indie music.
